All animals in need
in one map

There are a lot of animals that need to be rescued (sick animals, injured animals, hungry animals, pregnant animals that can’t move to eat, animals just give birth) in all the roads of the country, this web-site will enable to any person who find an animal in need and can’t help him, to put it localisation on the map and association will find him easely and help him.

About

What Inspired the Project

When I was a kid, I was an animal lover, and one day I was walking down the street and I saw a cat with a serious injury, I wanted to help him but I couldn’t because I was small and I couldn’t find anyone to ask him to help this poor cat then he died in front of my eyes at that time-there I was very sad and I wondered how many animals there are in this situation all over the world so I told myself when I grow up I will try to do something that can help these animals


Now that I had the opportunity to make an application web as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School I chose to make a site that allows people who find animals that need help to put an alert on the map with location, photo and description of the situation for someone to help them.
